Jiwon Park
Distorted interaction
2021
Worked with : Seongheum Na
Technique : P5JS, Arduino
"Distorted interaction" is an interactive web installation inspired by the music of composer John Cage.

The idea was to create an installation based on the deconstructivist music of John Cage, with interactivity to show unpredictable interactions. A team project that grew out of the project "WWVJ" (World Wide Visual Jockey), we created an interaction that integrated music and visuals.

To emphasize the deconstructionist motif of the work, we kept the interaction and installation as simple as possible.
